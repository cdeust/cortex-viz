**CitationTag** — pins a source to a claim, the way the brand always does. Dashed-underline mono tag that turns terracotta on hover; pass `href` to link the paper.

```jsx
Retrieved memories become labile. <CitationTag source="Nader et al. 2000" />
<CitationTag source="Friston 2005" href="https://…" />
```

Every number and mechanism in AI Architect copy should carry one of these.
